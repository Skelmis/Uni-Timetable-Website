Main Page & Details

The purpose of this website is to further make our univeristy timetables easy to use given the univeristies lack of tutorial and lab support within timetables unless approved by lecturers, as far as im aware.


This website allows access to timetables on any internet connected device so its perfect for any student on the go or at home wanting to see what they have on next, and with the choice of making your own lesson labels it means your timetable looks the way you want, not over cluttered like the one provided by the univeristy. For any example of what i mean search for the test user's timetable.
